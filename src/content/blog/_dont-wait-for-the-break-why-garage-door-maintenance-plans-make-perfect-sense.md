## How It Works: Certification & Eligibility

Not every door qualifies immediately. Here's what you need to know:

- **Initial Certification Inspection Required:** Your door must pass a Performance Certification Inspection to enroll. This ensures we're starting from a known baseline.
- **Pre-Existing Conditions:** If components are identified as "Poor" or "Failing" during certification, they're not covered until replaced. We're not taking on systems that are already compromised.
- **12-Month Minimum Commitment:** Early cancellation results in billing for provided parts/labor at retail rates. This is a care program, not a month-to-month discount card.
- **Exclusivity Clause:** If another company services your door, the membership is void. We can't guarantee performance if we're not the only ones working on the system.

### What's Not Covered

Performance Membership covers *wear*, not *damage*. That means:

- Forced operation, impact, obstruction, or abuse
- Door panels, tracks, opener motor/rail assemblies
- Cosmetic issues, rust, environmental corrosion
- Structural aging of the door itself

Membership applies **per door**. If you have multiple doors, each requires its own membership (though Estate Discounts apply).

**Selling your home?** The membership is fully transferable, which adds value for the next homeowner and can be a selling point in your listing.
